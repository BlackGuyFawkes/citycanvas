Artist Guidelines & Regulations

Size Restrictions

Artwork must not exceed 10ft x 10ft. Aisle space of 5ft must be maintained for pedestrian access at all times.

Approved Materials

Only water-soluble, non-permanent materials allowed. Oil-based paints and permanent markers are prohibited.

Time Restrictions

Artists may work between 8AM-8PM. Artwork must not obstruct pathways during rush hours (7-9AM, 4-6PM).

Prohibited Content

No commercial advertising, political statements, or content that could be considered offensive or discriminatory.

Renewal Process

Permits are valid for 7 days. Apply for renewal at least 3 days before expiration if you wish to continue.

Safety Requirements

Artists must provide their own safety cones and signage. Work areas must be clearly marked during creation.
